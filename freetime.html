Freetime

Traveling also plays a big role in my life. I’m always looking for ways to recharge and learn through new cultures, landscapes, and spontaneous adventures.

I’m also a vintage car enthusiast. Meet my Dodge Dart -64 GT — a personal project I’ve been restoring with care. I love combining craftsmanship with history under the hood.

Family plays a central role in my life. It’s the foundation of my support system and a source of daily motivation. Whether it’s spending time together outdoors, sharing routines, or simply being present — those are the most meaningful moments.
